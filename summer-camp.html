Summer Camp

Romanian Heritage Language Summer Camp

Presentation of the camp:

Our Dublin based Romanian Summer Camps are organised by Anka Borzasi, one of our Romanian language teachers. It's a two week activity-based language learning summer camp for post-primary level students. The camps will run from Monday to Friday between 10am and 3pm.

Students will be able to learn their native language and enrich their knowledge of Romanian culture through workshops, music auditions, cooking and handwork, linguistic games, debates, reading and writing, drawing and painting, dancing and sport activities (competitions, ball games, pilates).

You'll be able to meet other Romanian teenagers, be part of good company and have lots of fun.

Funding for the Heritage Language Summer Camps is provided from the Department of Education and Skills under the 10 year Languages Connect Strategy which was launched in December 2017, and the pilot scheme for summer camps is managed by Post-Primary Languages Initiative.

The curriculum of the camp includes:

  • Romanian Language and Communication
  • Romanian History
  • Romanian Geography
  • Romanian traditions and customs and workshop
  • Romanian traditional dances
  • Culinary Romania
  • Romanian Scientists
  • Famous Romanian athletes and sport activities
  • Important Romanian musicians and workshop
  • Romanian artists – Romanian art and workshop

You will learn:

  • to write correctly in Romanian
  • to communicate in Romanian on different topics
  • to improve your vocabulary
  • to learn the important events in the history of the Romanians
  • to learn the important relief forms of Romania and some of the most important tourist attractions of our country
  • to learn about traditional costumes
  • to sew handmade traditional objects
  • to learn traditional dances
  • to cook some traditional food
  • to learn about Romanian inventors and scientists
  • to learn about famous Romanian athletes and sportsmen
  • the history of Romanian music
  • to learn about famous Romanian artists and Romanian art

How to enroll?

The camp is only for for post-primary level students (ages 12-17). Places are limited and enrollment will be on a first come first served bases. The camp is free, but at registration you will have to pay in a €50 fee, money that will be returned on the last day of the camp. To enroll, please visit our facebook event page: Romanian Summer Camp
